# Investor One-Pager - Tile & Bloom

## Problem
Casual puzzle users churn quickly when games are either too hard, ad-heavy, or visually generic.

## Solution
Tile & Bloom: a cozy, female-friendly puzzle game with retention-first progression and fair monetization.

## Why Now
- Casual puzzle demand remains strong
- Short-form video channels reduce UA friction
- Lightweight teams can ship/prove metrics fast

## Product
- Match/tile puzzle core
- Daily rewards + events + progression
- Ads + IAP + future season pass

## Business Model
- Rewarded/interstitial ad revenue
- IAP packs (coins/boosters/no-ads)
- Later: pass & cosmetics

## KPI Targets
- D1: 35%+
- D7: 10%+
- ARPDAU: $0.03+ (starter)
- Crash-free users: 98%+

## Go-to-Market
- Organic creator clips (TikTok/Reels/Shorts)
- Play Store ASO optimization
- Staged paid UA once retention confirmed

## 90-Day Use of Funds
- Product polish
- Live-ops system
- Controlled UA scaling
