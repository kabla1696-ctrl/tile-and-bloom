# Levels 021-100 Progression Plan

## 021-040 (Easy+)
- Introduce 2nd blocker type
- Add timed pressure on selected levels
- Goal mix: collect + blockers + score
- Difficulty target: 70% win rate

## 041-060 (Medium)
- Tighter move limits
- Combo-dependent clears
- 1 “boss” level every 10 levels
- Difficulty target: 55-60% win rate

## 061-080 (Medium+)
- Double-layer blockers
- Multi-goal levels (2-3 simultaneous goals)
- Strategic booster use encouraged
- Difficulty target: 45-55% win rate

## 081-100 (Hard Intro)
- Puzzle-centric board layouts
- Low move count challenge
- Reward chest at L90 and L100
- Difficulty target: 40-50% win rate

## Design Rules
- Every 5th level = relief level
- Every 10th level = showcase/challenge
- Avoid 3 hard levels back-to-back
