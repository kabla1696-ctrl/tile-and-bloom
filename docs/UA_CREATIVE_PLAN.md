# User Acquisition Creative Plan (30 days)

## Channels
- TikTok
- Instagram Reels
- YouTube Shorts
- Google App Campaign (phase 2)

## Creative Angles (Top 5)
1. "Stress relief after long day" gameplay
2. "Satisfying combo" clips
3. "Daily rewards streak" challenge
4. "Can you beat this level?" challenge
5. "Cozy aesthetic" mood edits

## Ad Format
- 9:16 vertical
- 12-25 sec
- Hook in first 2 seconds
- Captions on screen
- End CTA: "Play free now"

## Posting Cadence
- 2 creatives/day (organic)
- 1 boosted post/day once baseline CTR found
- Refresh winning creatives every 5 days

## KPI Benchmarks (starter)
- CTR > 1.5%
- CPI target by geo (set weekly)
- Day-1 retention from paid > 30%
- Creative fatigue: replace if CTR drops 30%+
