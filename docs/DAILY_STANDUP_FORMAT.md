# Daily Standup Format (10 min)

## 1) Yesterday
- What was completed?
- What moved KPI forward?

## 2) Today
- Top 3 must-finish tasks
- Owner per task

## 3) Blockers
- What is blocked?
- Who will unblock it today?

## 4) KPI Snapshot
- D1 retention:
- Crash-free:
- Revenue yesterday:
- Top issue:

## 5) Decision of the Day
- One clear decision to avoid drift
