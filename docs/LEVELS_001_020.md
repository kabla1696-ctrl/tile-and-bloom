# Levels 001-020 (Starter Curve)

## L001-L005 (Tutorial)
- L001: Collect 6 red tiles, moves 15
- L002: Collect 6 blue tiles, moves 14
- L003: Collect 4 red + 4 blue, moves 14
- L004: Introduce combo x2, target score 1200, moves 16
- L005: Collect 5 green + 5 yellow, moves 15

## L006-L010 (Easy)
- L006: Score 1800, moves 17
- L007: Collect 8 purple, moves 15
- L008: Collect 5 red + 5 green + 5 blue, moves 18
- L009: Introduce blocker (1 layer), clear 6 blockers, moves 18
- L010: Mixed goal: score 2200 + clear 3 blockers, moves 19

## L011-L015 (Early Challenge)
- L011: Collect 10 yellow, moves 15
- L012: Score 2600, moves 16
- L013: Clear 8 blockers, moves 18
- L014: Collect 6 red + 6 blue + 6 green, moves 20
- L015: Combo objective: trigger 4 combos, moves 17

## L016-L020 (Retention Hook)
- L016: Score 3000, moves 18
- L017: Clear 10 blockers, moves 20
- L018: Collect 12 purple, moves 17
- L019: Mixed goal (score + collection), moves 21
- L020: Mini-boss level: tight board, moves 16, reward chest on win
