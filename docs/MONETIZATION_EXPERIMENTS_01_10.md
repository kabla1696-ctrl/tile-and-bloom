# Monetization Experiments (01-10)

## EXP-01 Rewarded Revive Placement
- Change: Show revive offer immediately on fail
- KPI: revive opt-in rate, D1 retention
- Guardrail: session drop rate

## EXP-02 Interstitial Frequency
- Change: every 2 levels vs every 3 levels
- KPI: ad ARPDAU
- Guardrail: D1 retention

## EXP-03 Starter Pack Timing
- Change: show after level 5 vs level 10
- KPI: IAP conversion
- Guardrail: uninstall rate (24h)

## EXP-04 No-Ads Offer Pricing
- Change: $2.99 vs $3.99
- KPI: no-ads purchase rate
- Guardrail: ARPPU

## EXP-05 Daily Reward Multiplier via Ad
- Change: x2 reward with ad
- KPI: rewarded impressions/user
- Guardrail: churn after reward screen

## EXP-06 Booster Economy
- Change: hint cost 60 -> 50 coins
- KPI: level completion rate
- Guardrail: coin inflation

## EXP-07 Chest Timer
- Change: free chest every 4h vs 6h
- KPI: session frequency
- Guardrail: ARPDAU

## EXP-08 Event Pass Intro
- Change: mini pass at day 7
- KPI: pass conversion
- Guardrail: D7 retention

## EXP-09 End-of-Level Offer
- Change: optional bundle popup on 3rd win streak
- KPI: IAP conversion
- Guardrail: UX dissatisfaction signal

## EXP-10 Segment-based Monetization
- Change: heavy users get value pack, new users get starter pack
- KPI: blended conversion + ARPDAU
- Guardrail: fairness / review sentiment
