# Meeting Rhythm

## Daily (10 min)
- Standup: yesterday/today/blockers
- One key metric callout

## Twice Weekly (30 min)
- Level difficulty + economy tuning review
- Monetization experiment review

## Weekly (60 min)
- Full scorecard review
- Priorities for next sprint
- Risk register updates

## Monthly (90 min)
- Strategic review
- Budget vs KPI alignment
- Scale / pivot decision
